About WeatherNow

This demo uses the Open-Meteo free API (no key required) for current conditions and forecasts, plus their geocoding service for city search.

  • Search any city, or use your location.
  • Toggle units °C / °F.
  • Glass UI inspired by modern weather apps.

Tip: Geolocation requires HTTPS or localhost.
